I am interested in the creation process from idea inception to design and prototyping all the way through to initial production. I try to create things whenever I get the time, ranging from random arduino gadgets and webapps to drawings and sculptures.

eat

I prepare 3 litres of the exact nutrition I require for the day every morning, and drink it throughout the day. I only really eat solid food socially, or when I feel like it (rare). I really think this is the holy grail of food solutions, especially for the average active college student. It allows for seamless nutrition tracking, takes a total of ~15 minutes a day to prepare and consume, can be carried around/stored very easily, is very cheap, and can be made to taste alright.

school

After changing my major 5 times over the course of 4 years at the University of Maryland, I finally settled with Information Science as perfect for me. It provides a relatively low workload (allowing me to focus on side projects and not end up forgetting about midterms) while managing to remain relevant to all my work interests. This means I should be able to work in the US on my student visa for up to 3 years after graduating doing things I am interested in.
